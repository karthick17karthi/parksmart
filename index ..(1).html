Our Motive

Park Smart aims to make city parking efficient, effortless, and eco-friendly. Our goal is to reduce traffic congestion, lower fuel waste, and provide a seamless parking experience using AI-powered tools, real-time monitoring, and smart bookings.

Features

Live Slot Status

Track real-time availability of parking spaces across city spots using camera sensors.

Instant Booking

Reserve your slot in seconds with email confirmation and entry QR code.

Auto Vehicle Detection

Detect vehicle number plates automatically at entry and exit using OpenCV.

Map Navigation

Get real-time directions to your reserved spot through integrated Google Maps.

Payment Integration

Pay securely via GPay scanner or QR code at the exit gate.

Chatbot + Voice Assistant

Ask queries or get help via our AI-powered chatbot and voice assistant.

How It Works

1
Open Park Smart and choose a parking location near you.
2
Sign in, enter your car details, and book your slot.
3
Follow Google Maps directions to your reserved parking space.
4
Pay using GPay/QR on exit. Simple, secure and contactless!
